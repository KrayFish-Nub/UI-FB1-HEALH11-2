Wind Energy üí®

Statement: Wind turbines can still make electricity even if there is no wind.

Logical Proposition: If there is no wind, then the turbine can still produce electricity.

P and Q: P = there is no wind, Q = turbine produces electricity

Truth Value: False

Scientific Explanation: Wind turbines depend on air movement. No wind means no blade rotation, hence no electricity.
